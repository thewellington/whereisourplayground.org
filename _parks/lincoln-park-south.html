Welcome

A half-generation of kids growing up near the South end of Lincoln Park have been without a playground they can easily access for almost six years. That is more than 2000 days! Each time Parks provides communication regarding this project it is only to push it back by another six months to a year. Here we are now {{ site.data.numbers.number-of-delays }} updates later and we seem no closer to breaking ground on the promised play area replacement.

It begs the question, is Seattle Parks being run in an efficient and effective manner? How are our taxpayer dollars being spent? How do we trust the word of Seattle Parks again?
